«Sky Planet Aviation Group» is open for pilots VATSIM and IVAO.

1. General Provisions.

1. «Sky Planet Aviation Group» is a community of aviation enthusiasts united by the desire to carry out virtual flights in the IVAO and VATSIM networks.

2.Sky Planet operates flights in accordance with the rules, guidelines and regulations of the IVAO and VATSIM networks.

3. We do not require a thorough knowledge of the flight manual and procedures for aircraft types, aerodynamics, air navigation, meteorology and other disciplines. There are enough materials on the net for full self-education.

4.All materials published on the VA website are intended only for virtual flights and can in no way be applied in real aviation.

2. Flight operations.

1. Flights are made in flight simulators Microsoft Flight Simulator, Prepar3D or X-Plane and only in online mode. Simultaneous flight in two nets is not allowed.

2. Pilots are not allowed to turn on acceleration during the flight, although information about the performed flight is saved, it is not included in the pilot's flight time and airline statistics.

3. Flight rules.

1. All pilots of the VA are obliged to comply with the rules of flights in the "IVAO" and "VATSIM" networks.

2. All VA pilots are obliged to comply with all instructions issued by the air traffic controller in a timely manner.

3. In the absence of dispatch control, pilots must use the Unicom frequency, namely, a report on their actions in text and an assessment of messages from other pilots, if there is at least one aircraft at the airport of arrival or departure. The use of Unicom is mandatory for all VA pilots, as it is part of radio circumspection, which directly affects flight safety. If this rule is ignored, various punishments can be applied to the pilot, up to and including expulsion from Sky Planet.

4. The maximum aircraft reservation time is 24 hours. That is, if during this time the return flight is not performed by the pilot who performed the first flight (outbound flight), subject to the reservation of the return flight by this crew, any pilot has the right to take the board. If the board was not reserved by the pilot who made the first flight, then any other pilot can reserve it.

5. It is forbidden to abuse booking, that is, to reserve a board, knowing in advance that the flight will not be operated. For pilots noticed in such violations, sanctions will be applied up to expulsion from VA! ("Dear friends, if you know that you will not fly on the return flight, then you should not deprive other pilots (your colleagues) to perform it for you. We are all one team and must respect each other!")

4. Pilot regulations.

1. An application to join the VA can be submitted by any person who is registered in the "IVAO" and "VATSIM" networks.

2. All VA pilots are required to fly only through booking on this site, with the exception of group flights, charter flights and training flights, in which case it is necessary to use the pilot's personal callsign. For example: AFL7000

3. In case of gross violation by the current pilot of the Charter or Flight Operations Manual of «Sky Planet Aviation Group», in case of abuse of violations, intolerant behavior towards other pilots, dispatchers or virtual airlines of the VATSIM or IVAO system, or other other public resources dedicated to the VATSIM and VATRUS systems the pilot can be expelled from the Sky Planet Higher Attestation Commission.

Flight Details
Preparation of Aeroflot Agent software and flight rules.

The Aeroflot Agent client software is designed for flights in the virtual airline «Sky Planet Aviation Group» Flights are operated in the "IVAO" and "VATSIM" networks. Selecting and booking a flight, viewing statistics and current information, and other operations are performed on the site. The Aeroflot Agent client is designed to send more detailed information about the flight to the site and display the flight status.

List of monitored flight parameters:
  1. Simulator speed (acceleration prohibited).
  2. Limit pitch angle during takeoff and landing.
  3. Putting the aircraft into stall mode (by the signal from the simulator).
  4. Exceeding the maximum flight speed (by the signal from the simulator).
  5. Refueling in the air (Refueling in the air is prohibited).
  6. The amount of fuel on board during take-off and landing.
  7. Time of departure and arrival in UTC.
  8. Vertical landing speed at the moment of touchdown.
Landing count Yes:
  • excellent - with vertical speed in the range of 80-240 ft / min.
  • hard - with vertical speed over 500 fpm.
  • emergency - with vertical speed over 700 fpm.
  • catastrophe - at vertical speed over 1600 fpm.
Bonus points
  1. Bonus +2 points for a long flight
    Plus to the assessment for a long-distance flight. The longer the flight, the greater the bonus.
  2. Bonus for excellent landing +10 points
    Plus to the rating for landing with a vertical speed in the range of 80-240 ft / min.
  3. Bonus for order +10 points
    Plus to the mark for returning the aircraft to the base airport
Minus points
  1. Penalty for side wreck - 99 points
    Minus the mark for the destruction of an aircraft in an accident.Crash is determined by a 'CRASH' signal from FSUIPC or by hitting the ground with a vertical speed of more than 1600 fpm.
  2. Penalty for stalls -20 points
    Minus the score for the withdrawal of the aircraft for stall mode.
  3. Penalty for speeding -5 points
    Minus the mark for exceeding the speed limit recorded by the simulator.
  4. Penalty for hard landing -5 points
    Less rough landing score.
What we need to fly:
  1. Microsoft Flight Simulator, Prepar3D or X-Plane .
  2. Registered version of FSUIPC or XPUIPC.
  3. Client for flights in VATSIM or IVAO network.
  4. Current installed AIRAC.
  5. Pilot client Aeroflot Agent - available for download on website.
How to book a flight:

To book a flight, go to the schedule menu, select the flight we need, and go to the page "Select an airliner". Select the desired aircraft, click "Available" and go to the page "Booking Details" (Briefing) It is possible to change the departure time, flight level, alternate airport in the briefing, then click "Book". After booking, click "Send FP" to Vatsim.

Launching the Aeroflot agent:

The Aeroflot agent is not installed, but simply starts (we recommend launching the Aeroflot agent and the simulator on behalf of the administrator) When you first start the client, enter the username and password that you specified during registration. After you have booked a flight through the site, the client will automatically see your booking, you just have to press the "Fly" button and you can fly.After arriving at the parking lot, press the "Send Flight" button on the client
